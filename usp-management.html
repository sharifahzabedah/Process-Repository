End to end view of USP management from Invitation to tender up to claim reconciliation.

Process Breakdown (L0 & L1)

Click each L1 activity below to expand its detailed L2 process steps.

Objective: To formally request a good or service and obtain initial approval.

L2 Process Steps:

  1. 1.1 Identify Need: Requester identifies a business need for a good or service.
  2. 1.2 Create Purchase Requisition (PR): Requester logs into the ERP system and completes the PR form (p/n, quantity, justification).
  3. 1.3 Submit for Approval: Requester submits the PR to their direct manager for budget approval.

Objective: To source the approved request and issue a formal Purchase Order (PO) to the vendor.

L2 Process Steps:

  1. 2.1 Manager Approval: Manager reviews the PR, checks budget, and either approves or rejects it.
  2. 2.2 Sourcing Review: (If > $10k) Procurement team receives the approved PR and identifies 3 potential vendors.
  3. 2.3 Vendor Selection: Procurement selects the vendor based on price, quality, and delivery time.
  4. 2.4 Create Purchase Order (PO): Procurement creates a formal PO from the PR and issues it to the selected vendor.

L2 Process Steps:

  1. 3.1 Goods Receipt: Receiving department accepts delivery and performs a 2-way match (Goods vs. PO).
  2. 3.2 Invoice Receipt: AP receives vendor invoice.
  3. 3.3 3-Way Match: AP performs 3-way match (PO vs. Goods Receipt vs. Invoice).

L2 Process Steps:

  1. 4.1 Approve for Payment: AP approves matched invoice.
  2. 4.2 Payment Run: AP schedules and executes the payment run (e.g., weekly ACH).
  3. 4.3 Post to GL: Payment is posted to the General Ledger.

Service Level Agreements (SLAs) & KPIs

  • PR Approval Time (SLA): < 2 business days
  • PO Issuance Time (SLA): < 1 business day from approved PR
  • Invoice Processing Time (SLA): < 5 business days from 3-way match
  • KPI: Days Payable Outstanding (DPO)
  • KPI: On-Time Payment Rate (> 98%)
